Merry Christmas

This gift holds my failings for the year.

My first failing, and the least important, is that I couldn’t get a job, and have no money. I’m sorry, I didn’t have money to buy you a nice Christmas gift this year, and so I decided to make one instead.

But my failure to find a job is minuscule in comparison to my other failing, which is my failure to remain faithful to you. I know this has been my biggest failing. And if I could put a picture to this year it is a cactus because there is hurt everywhere. I’ve put you through hell, and no matter where you turned there was pain.

And when you needed me, to make the pain go away, I wasn’t there.

You’ve been crying out asking me to take more ownership of this situation, and my behavior, and I haven’t. I’ve been running away because facing yourself is hard, and it hurts. I’m sorry I was such a weak person this year. I’m sorry I didn’t have the character and courage to deal with my problems. That I have been someone who dishonored you, and who you are ashamed of. I’m sorry that I made going home so hard for you, that I turned a safe place into a place filled with hurt. I’m sorry that I refused to listen to you.
